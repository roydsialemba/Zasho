United for Impact: The Skilled Team Behind ZASHO’s Success

Litrudah Chiila

The Founder and Executive Director, is a seasoned professional with expertise in Sexual Reproductive Health, Environmental Education, and Peace and Conflict Management. She is the Executive Director of Zambian Adolteens Sustainable Health Organisation (ZASHO) and serves as a Gender Specialist at African Mining Consultancy on the World Bank-funded Zambia-Tanzania Interconnector Project. With 22 years of teaching experience, Litruda has also been an Adjunct Lecturer and project coordinator. She holds a bachelor's degree in Education, a Master's in Peace and Conflict, and is currently pursuing a PhD in Geography and Environmental Studies at the University of the Witwatersrand.

He saved as CSPR Board Chairperson for 4 years and is currently the Board Chairperson of Zitukule Self Help Consortium.
